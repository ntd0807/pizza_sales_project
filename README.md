# pizza_sales_project
This repository includes 3 documents for the project: SQL file for EDA, Excel File for visualization and Word for validating purpose.

One of the most interesting parts of this project was designing the Hourly Trend analysis, which clearly shows when customers are most active.

Some key insights:

~45.5% of total revenue is generated during evening hours (5 PM–11 PM), making it the strongest sales period.
Lunch hours (11 AM–2 PM) contribute ~39% of revenue, showing a strong secondary peak in demand.
The top 5 products account for ~24.5% of total revenue, indicating moderate product concentration.
Customers order an average of 2.32 items per transaction, highlighting consistent upselling behavior.
Interestingly, weekday sales slightly outperform weekends (~6.6% higher on average), suggesting demand is more routine-driven than leisure-driven.

Based on these insights, a couple of potential business actions:

Focus promotions and staffing around evening peak hours to maximize revenue and reduce service bottlenecks.
Introduce bundling or upselling strategies during lunch periods to increase average order value.
